How to Play

Fred the baker is retiring, but his passion for donuts still burns bright. He invented the Donut Maker 9000, and put you in charge of it. Luckily he built an auto clicker button that will make donuts for you every second. Before Fred hung up his apron you did hear him say he couldn't wait for "Total Donut World Domination!" You aren't sure what that is about, but I doubt you need to worry.

  1. Click the "Click for Donut" button to increase the number of donuts.
  2. Click on "Buy Auto Clicker" button to purchase an auto clicker
    • For each auto clicker you have purchased an additional donut will be made per second.
  3. Click the "Reset" button to start the game from the beginning (0 donuts)
